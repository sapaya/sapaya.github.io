My Projects

Here is a selection of my personal projects. Check my github to see what else I'm working on. You can click the image to open the git repo for each project.

Coming Home

A 3D point and click puzzle game, focused on atmosphere and subtle storytelling. My role is as one of two developers, working on all parts of the game. Coming Home won the special jury prize at the Games Awards Saar 2019.

HRSSystem

My bachlor thesis, exploring how to tell the same story through different mediums: VR, mobile comic and video. Main focus was making the connection between comics and VR, and how to use the sequential image format of comics effectively to tell a story in VR. I produced both the code and all assets for the project.

Cat Food Block

A small game to hone my love for cats and casual gaming. I was the sole programmer and made all assets.
